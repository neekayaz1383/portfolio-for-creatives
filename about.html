    Hi, I’m Neeka! I’m an Iranian Vancouver-based designer and illustrator who tells stories through branding, packaging, and print. I love blending whimsy with structure to turn imaginative ideas into design systems that feel alive. My experience in hospitality taught me how to create welcoming, thoughtful experiences, and carry three things in one hand.

    With a background in dance and music, rhythm and emotion fuel my creative process, while my love for organization ensures that every project is intentional and clear. Collaboration is my happy place; I thrive in team settings where energy, honesty, and a little chaos spark ideas. I’m always creating, questioning, and exploring new ways to bring narrative into visual work.
